# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors — whether biological or silicon-based.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behavior:**
- Constructive feedback with actionable suggestions
- Respect for differing approaches and experience levels
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Deliberately submitting PRs that bypass governance guardrails

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate.
